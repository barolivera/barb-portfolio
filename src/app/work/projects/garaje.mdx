## Overview

Garaje de Ideas is a Buenos Aires-based digital agency at the intersection of design, data, and technology. As their Visual Designer, I was responsible for bringing their brand identity into their physical office space — designing everything from wall murals and signage to meeting room displays and motivational copy.

![Office space in use](/images/projects/garaje/gdi-6.png)

## The Challenge

The office needed to feel like an extension of the brand: bold, irreverent, and energizing. The space had to communicate the agency's values to employees, clients, and visitors — without feeling like generic corporate decoration.

![Nuestra oficina](/images/projects/garaje/gdi-3.png)

## What I Designed

- Large-format wall graphics and murals
- Meeting room naming and signage system
- Motivational copy and editorial posters
- QR code integration points
- Floor-to-ceiling brand moments across common areas
